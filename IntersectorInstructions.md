# Intersector Help

## Nearest Intersection

Pressing this button or using the Siri Shortcut will find and show the intersection that is the closest to your location. VoiceOver actions and a visible icon on the button allows you to find the 2nd and 3rd closest intersections around you based on distance.

Note: the visible menu icon can be hidden in Settings if it is distracting.

You are able to change how these announcements appear and are spoken by VoiceOver, but in general Intersector will say the street you are on first followed by the cross street. If the intersection is split, such as having a different street on the left from the street on the right, Intersector will speak the street you are on first followed by the left street and then the right street as if you were facing the intersection.

## Upcoming Intersection

This feature will speak the intersection that is directly in front of you while you are moving, or will speak whichever intersection is immediately in front of wherever you are pointing your phone. Using the 2nd Upcoming Intersection action will speak whichever intersection comes after this first one along your path of travel. The 3rd Upcoming will be the intersection that appears after the 2nd one if you were to continue following your current street.

Note: the visible menu icon for this button to show the 2nd and 3rd Upcoming features can be hidden in Settings.


## Speak Direction

This will show and speak the current cardinal direction that your phone is facing.

## Point and Scan

Turning this mode on will activate a live experience where physically pointing your phone around will identify intersections in your immediate vicinity and up to 600 feet away. Light haptic pulses will get stronger the closer you are pointing your phone towards an intersection, and will emanate a strong pulse and speak the intersection information once the top of the phone is pointing directly at it.
